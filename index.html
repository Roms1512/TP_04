About

FAICCO'S Restaurant was opened by award winning Chel Luciano Canova to ofter residents and visitors of southerm Brooklyn with the authentic italian cuisine that the Faicco's name has become synonymous with after the success of the Faicco's restaurant in Las Vegas. The restaurant now has a loyal following of it's own, offering traditionnal Roman recipes passed down from Chef Luciano's family. as well as inventive Northerm and Southerm itailian preparations inspired by Chef Luciano's culiriary education and travels.
